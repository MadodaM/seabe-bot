Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Seabe Digital ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our WhatsApp Payment Bot.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone Number: Required to identify you on WhatsApp and link your donations to your profile.
  • Email Address: Optional. Used solely to send tax certificates (Section 18A) or payment receipts.
  • Transaction Data: We store the amount, date, and reference of your donations for historical records.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Process your tithes and offerings securely.
  • Generate and send PDF receipts to your WhatsApp.
  • Provide your church with financial reports (names and amounts).

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your data. We share data only with the following trusted providers to function:

  • Paystack: Processes all payments securely. We do not store your card details.
  • Twilio: Facilitates the WhatsApp messaging service.
  • Render & Neon: Hosting and database providers for our secure infrastructure.

4. Your Rights (POPIA)

You have the right to request access to your data or ask for it to be deleted. To do so, please contact us below.
